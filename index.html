Focus Areas

Our foundation's activities and programs are primarily focused in the areas of education and technology research. We aim to create some 'first-of-its-kind' programs through our foundation whereby our industry and academic partners can collectively come together to solve societal problems.

Analytics for Agriculture

We aim to carry out data analytics projects focused on addressing agriculture problems in the fields of our State

Research on Data Quality

Data quality has been and still will be very pivotal for future smart solutions. Our pilot programs focus on addressing this critical issue.

Education Support Program

Outreach & financial assistance to school and college students pursuing arts, science or applied science curriculum

About I-Stem

1

Vision

Our vision encompasses learning, evaluating and addressing the collective needs of our society for developing a sustainable future, and to equip, to our best possible extent, the youth with the necessary futuristic knowledge, skills and scientific temper. 

2

Mission

- To initiate, lead and implement projects in the STEM fields which will result in novel and state-of-the-art solutions which directly benefit our society.

- To promote and establish partnerships with educational institutions to carry out technical and non-technical programmes for the betterment of the students and society. 

- To propose structured and pragmatic solutions to address the lack of new-age skills amongst the current and future workforce of the country.

3

Social 

We firmly believe that focusing on social factors is key to any transformative development.  In this regard, we aim to initiate social programs which directly impact children from rural areas and financially weaker sections.
